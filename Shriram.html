Universal Sompo Home Insurance

Universal Sompo Home Insurance is a comprehensive insurance policy offered by Universal Sompo General Insurance Company that provides protection for your home and its contents against various risks. This insurance policy is designed to safeguard your property from potential damages caused by natural disasters, fire, theft, and other unforeseen events.

Features and Benefits:

  • Coverage for the structure
  • Coverage for contents
  • Natural Disaster Coverage
  • Fire and Allied Perils
  • Theft and Burglary Protection
  • Additional Coverage Options
  • Liability Coverage
  • Affordable Premiums

Why purchase Universal Sompo Home Insurance Policy?

  • Protection is provided for the customers property and all the contents within it, all encompassed within a single policy plan
  • Universal Sompo offers very competitive and attractive premium rates for this insurance
  • A wide range of assets and items are covered under the single all-inclusive Householder's Insurance plan, ranging from jewellary and ornaments to laptops

Scope of coverage of the Universal sompo Home Insurance Plan

  • Fired Allied Perils
  • Burglary and Robbery
  • All Risk
  • Plate Glass Cover
  • Electronic Equipments
  • Electrical Mechanical Breakdown of Domestic appliances
  • Personal Accident Cover
  • Baggage Insurance
  • Workmen's Compension
  • Public Liability Cover

What is not covered under the Universal Sompo Home Insurance Plans?

  • Damage and loss that is caused to money cheques / stamps / currency.
  • Damage and loss to motor vehicles, pedal cycles, and livestock are not covered.
  • When there is damage caused to audio-visual equipment for which the manufacturer or supplier has to take responsibility, then the policy will not cover it.
  • When mechanical and electronic equipment is willfully damaged or caused damage to by gross negligence then it will not be covered.
  • Mobile phones and other such communication gadgets are not covered by the policy.
  • The personal accident cover has a few exclusions of its own.
  • When the injury has been intentionally caused to the self.
  • When alcohol and drugs are the cause of the accident.
  • Illegal acts or violation of law or the person resistance to arrest.
  • Accidents that have been caused bu curative treatments and interventions.
  • Any complications that arise out of pregnancy, miscarriage, abortion and childbirth.
  • Radiation and nuclear contamination are not covered.
  • Pre-existing disabilities are not covered.
  • Venereal diseases and sexually transmitted diseases, HIV / AIDS are not covered.
  • A hospital stay that is not specifically recommended by a doctor.
  • Expenses that are non-medical in nature and emergency medical evacuations are not covered.
  • Loss and Damage caused due to war
  • Nuclear war
